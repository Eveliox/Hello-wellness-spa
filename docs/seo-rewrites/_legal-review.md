## Cross-cutting risks

1. **§503A compounding — GLP-1s + multiple peptides** — highest cross-cutting exposure. Post-Oct 2024 FDA shortage-list resolution restricted §503A GLP-1 copies; BPC-157/TB-500 on FDA "significant concern" list from 2023. Current draft treats §503A as normal ongoing option — out of step with post-2024 reality. Outside counsel review required.
2. **"Physician-led" vs "physician-supervised" vs "administered by physician"** — multiple pages describe clinic as "physician-led" without specifying the actual delegation model. Audit every use.
3. **"Starts at" pricing without inclusions at point of claim** — every price needs same-glance disclosure.
4. **"Free consultation" — actually free?** Must be truly free or reworded.
5. **"Same-week / same-day / often available" substantiation** — needs data-backed defense or softening.
6. **Trademark acknowledgment gap** — Wegovy, Ozempic, Zepbound, Mounjaro, Botox, Dysport, Xeomin, Jeuveau, Daxxify, Juvederm, Restylane, RHA, Radiesse, Sculptra, Bellafill, Skinvive, Vyleesi, Egrifta, Geref all appear unqualified. Add site-wide footer + ® on first mention per page.
7. **Off-label marketing (distinct from off-label prescribing)** — appears on aesthetics (Sculptra/Radiesse for buttocks), peptide (PT-141 for men, sermorelin/tesamorelin in wellness), hormone (women's testosterone). Physicians may prescribe off-label; marketing off-label uses publicly is FDA-actionable.
8. **Individual-results-vary disclaimer** — inconsistent. Should appear adjacent to every efficacy claim.
9. **Telehealth disclosures under Fla. Stat. §456.47** — weight loss, IV, hormone pages mention telehealth without a Telehealth Disclosures page.
10. **HIPAA on website** — if Meta Pixel, GA, or 3rd-party tracking runs on pages collecting PHI (intake, portal, booking), Dec 2022 HHS OCR guidance applies. Sitemap review needed.
11. **Spanish/English claim consistency** — any drift is a Florida DBPR deceptive-trade risk.
12. **"Longevity," "anti-aging"** — mostly avoided; one drift in `iv-therapy.md` L131 ("longevity protocols"). Scrub globally.
