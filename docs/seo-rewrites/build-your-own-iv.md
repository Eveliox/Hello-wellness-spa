## How It Works

Building your own IV at Hello You Wellness Center is a three-step process, designed so you have control without shouldering the clinical judgment.

1. **Browse add-ons.** Start from the catalog below or bring a goal — energy, immunity, skin glow, athletic recovery. Every add-on lists what it is, what it is commonly used for, and how it pairs with other ingredients.
2. **Clinician review.** Your proposed formula is reviewed by our clinical team under physician oversight before it is compounded. If a combination is not appropriate for your history, we will adjust and explain why.
3. **Administer.** A licensed nurse places the IV in our private lounge suite. First-time custom builds add roughly ten minutes to allow for review and confirmation. Your final formula is saved digitally for future visits.

## IV Base Selection

Every custom build starts with a base fluid. The base is chosen by the clinician based on your build, hydration status, and medical history — not selected from a menu — but it helps to know the options.

- **Normal saline (0.9% sodium chloride).** The default base for most infusions. Isotonic and broadly compatible.
- **Lactated ringers.** An electrolyte-balanced base often preferred for athletic recovery and post-exertion builds. Contains sodium, potassium, calcium, and lactate.
- **Half-normal saline (0.45% sodium chloride).** Used in specific clinical scenarios where a lower sodium load is preferred. [CLINICAL REVIEW]

Your provider will explain which base is used for your build and why.

### How is my custom blend medically reviewed?
Every Build Your Own IV formula is reviewed by our clinical team under physician oversight before it is compounded. The review looks at your medical history, current medications, and the interactions and dose ranges of the specific nutrients you selected. If a change is needed, we explain why and offer alternatives. Nothing is prepared until a licensed provider signs off.

### What's the difference between a Myers' Cocktail and a custom IV?
A Myers' Cocktail is a fixed, well-studied formulation — B-complex, B12, vitamin C, magnesium, and calcium — that has been used in clinical settings for decades. A custom IV lets you start from a base and select add-ons aligned to a specific goal, with clinician review before compounding. If you are new to IV therapy, a Myers' Cocktail is often the easier starting point. If you have a specific goal in mind or you are refining a formula over multiple visits, a custom build gives you more control.
