# Front Desk SOP — Reviews + Memberships

**Purpose:** Give the front desk one page they can actually follow. Two moments matter more than anything else: the moment a guest checks in, and the moment a guest checks out. This SOP tells you exactly what to say and do in each.

**Owner:** [Front desk manager]
**Review cadence:** Monthly

---

## The two moments

### Check-in (goal: prime for the review)
- Warm greeting by name.
- Confirm intake is done (portal or paper).
- If they arrived early: offer water, mention "if you have a great visit today, we'd love a Google review after."

### Check-out (goal: send the review request + pitch membership)
Every guest, every time. Two things:

1. **Send the review link.** Open [admin panel](https://helloyouwellness.com/admin) → "Send review request" form → enter their name + phone + service → click Send. They get a text within 30 seconds.

2. **Membership pitch, if it fits.** Read the room. If they enjoyed the visit and mentioned they'd come back, mention memberships. Never pitch if they seemed rushed or unhappy.

---

## Review request — scripts

### Standard ask (script A)

> "Before you go — mind if I text you a quick 30-second feedback link? It's just so we know how we did. If you loved it, it'll take you to Google to share. If anything wasn't great, it comes straight to us privately."

**If yes:** send via admin panel (below).
**If no:** never push. Say "totally get it, thanks for coming in."

### If they hesitate (script B)

> "No worries. I'll send it anyway in case you feel like it later. Zero pressure."

Send it. Some people fill it out later.

### If they say "I don't do Google" (script C)

> "That's fine. Our form isn't public. If you liked it, you can text me directly what worked — or just close the tab."

Send it. The private form still helps us track NPS.

---

## Sending the review request

1. Go to https://helloyouwellness.com/admin
2. Log in (ask [owner] for password)
3. Fill in:
   - Patient name
   - Phone (US only)
   - Email (optional but nice)
   - Service dropdown (pick the one they had)
4. Click **Send review request**
5. Two things can happen:
   - Green banner → SMS sent, you're done
   - Beige banner → SMS not configured (or failed). Copy the link, open WhatsApp or your personal text, paste it and send.

**Do not send the same person twice within 30 days.** If they already got a link, skip and note it.

## Membership pitch — scripts

### When to pitch
- Guest enjoyed the visit and said they'd come back
- Guest asked about pricing on more than one service
- Guest booked a repeat appointment
- Guest complimented the space, provider, or approach

### When NOT to pitch
- Guest seemed rushed
- Guest expressed any dissatisfaction (fix that first)
- Guest is a first-time consult who didn't get a treatment yet

### Script A — the math pitch

> "One thing I always mention — if you're planning to come back at all, memberships pay for themselves in one visit. Renew is $149/month, includes a full IV every month plus a B-12 shot, plus 20% off everything else. Do you want me to send you the breakdown?"

- If yes → text them https://helloyouwellness.com/memberships
- If yes and they want to sign up now → walk them to the tablet, hit Renew, they enroll on the spot.

### Script B — the specific service pitch

> "Since you loved the [facial / IV / whatever], Revive members get one of those every month included. If you'd do that anyway, the membership pays for itself. Want me to text you the link?"

### Script C — the low-effort pitch (default)

> "Any interest in memberships? We have three tiers depending on how often you're planning to come in. Happy to text you the link so you can look later."

Send https://helloyouwellness.com/memberships either way.

## Handling objections

**"I don't want to commit."**
- 3-month minimum, then cancel any time. Pause up to 1 month/year. Real answer: it's the lowest-friction commitment we can offer while still making the math work for everyone.

**"It's too expensive."**
- Refresh is $79 and includes a B-12 or lipotropic (worth $35–45) plus 15% off everything. If you're coming in once, you basically break even.

**"I don't come in often enough."**
- Rollover: if you don't use your IV or facial credit this month (Renew or Revive), it carries into next. So even every-other-month works.

**"Let me think about it."**
- "Totally. I'll text you the link now — no pressure." Send it. Do not push.

## Daily checklist for the front desk

At end of day:

- [ ] Every guest who checked out today got a review link sent (or noted why not)
- [ ] Any membership signups logged in [wherever mom tracks them]
- [ ] Any escalation emails from feedback engine flagged to [owner]
- [ ] Tomorrow's schedule reviewed — do any first-time patients need extra care?

---

## Weekly checklist for the manager

Every Monday:

- [ ] Check the Monday digest email — how did last week go? Any unresolved escalations?
- [ ] Reply to every unreplied Google review (aim for <48h response time)
- [ ] Note the week's NPS number somewhere trackable
- [ ] Any repeated feedback themes? Flag them to the team meeting

---

## Do not ever

- Ask a guest for a Google review directly (Google TOS violates soliciting reviews outside their platform → we go through our NPS gate instead)
- Send a review link to someone who didn't come in
- Pitch membership to an unhappy guest
- Use the same script robotically — read the person in front of you first

## When to escalate to the owner

- Guest says anything about a bad reaction, side effect, or medical concern → call [owner] immediately
- Guest is upset → apologize, offer to have [owner] call them back within the hour, do NOT try to resolve on the spot
- Google review with 3 stars or fewer posted → reply publicly and text [owner] the link
