# Google Business Profile Optimization Walkthrough

**Why this matters:** Google Business Profile (GBP) is the #1 SEO asset for a local business. The website matters, but the GBP shows up first in Google Maps and the Local Pack — which is where 70%+ of local searches convert.

Do this in order. Each section takes 15–30 minutes.

---

## 1. Claim + verify (do first, if not already done)

1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Search for "Hello You Wellness Center" and claim the listing (or add if not present)
3. Verify (usually postcard by mail to the SW Miami address — takes 5–14 days)
4. Once verified, continue below.

## 4. Photos — the highest-ROI activity (30 min + ongoing)

**Goal:** 20+ photos this month. Update monthly with fresh shots.

Take these:

- **Exterior (3–5):** Storefront from the street. Sign visible. Different times of day. Head-on + angle shots. **This is what Google weights most for Local Pack.**
- **Interior (5–8):** Reception, waiting area, treatment rooms (empty and clean), consult room, product display.
- **Team (2–4):** Noel, Yanelys, front desk — professional, but warm. Include name in the caption.
- **Product close-ups (3–5):** IV setup, branded vials, peptide packaging, membership tier card.
- **Real moments (with consent, 2–3):** A patient in an IV chair (from behind), a provider mid-consult (staged is fine), the calm before a treatment starts.
- **Logo:** Cover image + profile image. Cream background with charcoal logotype.

**Photo specs Google prefers:**
- 720×720 minimum
- JPG or PNG
- Under 5MB each
- No stock imagery
- No logos overlaid on other photos
- No text overlays (Google may reject)

## 6. Q&A — 15 min

Google shows a Q&A section on every profile. Seed it yourself so competitors don't seed it for you.

Post these questions (from a personal Google account, not the business account), then answer from the business account:

1. "Do you take walk-ins?" → "Walk-ins are welcome for IV therapy when we have availability. All other services require a booked appointment. Call (786) 780-3626 or book online."
2. "Do you offer free consultations?" → "Yes. All weight loss, hormone therapy, aesthetics, and Galleri consults are complimentary. No pressure to book same-day."
3. "Are the providers licensed?" → "Every treatment is delivered or supervised by a licensed APRN under physician supervision."
4. "Do you have memberships?" → "Yes — three tiers starting at $79/month. Monthly IV or injections included plus 15–25% off everything. See helloyouwellness.com/memberships"
5. "Do you speak Spanish?" → "Sí, tenemos personal bilingüe. Bilingual staff always available."

## 7. Weekly posts — ongoing

Google prioritizes profiles that post regularly. Post at least once a week.

**Post types to rotate:**
- **Offer post** — "This week: same-week Galleri consults available"
- **Event post** — "Members Q&A this Thursday at 6pm — DM to join"
- **Update post** — "New service: mobile IV across Miami — we come to you"
- **Product post** — highlight a specific treatment or peptide

**Include:**
- Photo (existing GBP photo or new one)
- 50–150 word description
- CTA button (Book, Learn more, Call)
- Link to the relevant page on helloyouwellness.com

## 8. Reviews — the compounding lever

**Every week:**
- Reply to every new review within 48 hours (this is a ranking signal).
- Response templates:
  - **5-star:** "Thank you for the kind words, [name]! Grateful to have you as part of the Hello You family — see you at your next visit."
  - **4-star:** "Thank you, [name]! We appreciate the honest feedback. If there's anything specific we can do better, we'd love to hear it — [owner]@helloyouwellness.com."
  - **≤ 3-star:** REPLY PUBLICLY: "Thank you for taking the time to share this. This isn't the experience we want anyone to have. Please email [owner]@helloyouwellness.com or call (786) 780-3626 — we want to make it right." Then also reach out privately.

**Every month:**
- Count new reviews. Goal: 10+/month until we hit 50 total.
- Note the star average.
- If the average dips below 4.7, meet with the team.

## 9. Monthly maintenance — 15 min per month

- [ ] Add 5+ new photos
- [ ] Post 4+ times in the week
- [ ] Reply to every review from the past month
- [ ] Check for new Google attributes to enable
- [ ] Confirm hours are correct (holidays, staff changes)
- [ ] Screenshot the "Insights" tab: track calls, direction requests, and website clicks

---

## 10. What NOT to do

- Do not solicit reviews with incentives (Google will suspend the profile — $50 for a review is a violation).
- Do not stuff the business name with keywords ("Hello You Wellness Center - Best Miami Med Spa" will get flagged).
- Do not use stock photos.
- Do not fake Q&A answers (a real customer will call you out).
- Do not respond to reviews with a template that's obviously copy-pasted — Google's algorithm can detect this.
