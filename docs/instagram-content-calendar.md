# Instagram Content Calendar — 30 Posts

**Purpose:** 6 weeks of ready-to-post content so mom's team doesn't stare at a blank page. Post 5x/week (Mon–Fri), one from each pillar plus a wildcard. Adjust to the week's clinic reality.

## The 4 pillars

| Pillar | Weekly cadence | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Provider POV / Trust** (Noel & Yanelys) | 2× per week | Med spa buyers need a face to trust before booking. |
| **2. Education** (GLP-1, Galleri, hormones, IV, aesthetics) | 2× per week | Positions you as authority. Wins DMs + saves. |
| **3. Patient stories + before/afters** (with consent) | 1× per week | Highest conversion driver. |
| **4. Behind-the-glass + promos** | 1× per week | Brand + community. |

**Format mix (2026 IG algorithm):** 60% Reels · 25% carousels · 15% single posts. Stories daily (poll/quiz/DM sticker).

**Design system:** Cream (#f7f7f7) or charcoal (#212020) backgrounds. Blush (#E8B4A3) accent only. Fraunces for display, Jakarta for body, Alex Brush for signature quotes. No random stock imagery — either provider POV or clinic/product close-ups.

## Do NOT post

- Weight loss before/afters (Google + Meta policy risk)
- Numbers-focused body outcomes ("lost 40 lbs!")
- Any "guaranteed" language
- Drug brand names (Ozempic, Wegovy) in captions
- Anything that references a specific patient without written consent

## Escalation flow

- Question in DM about medical eligibility → route to consult booking
- Question in DM about pricing → give range, always route to consult
- Any medical complaint or concern → immediately DM route to phone
